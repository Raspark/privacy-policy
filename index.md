# 隱私政策 Privacy Policy

**最後更新日期 Last Updated: 2026-02-03**

歡迎使用「Polify」（以下簡稱「本 App」）。我們重視您的隱私權，並承諾依照相關法規保護您的個人資料。本隱私政策說明我們如何處理資訊，以便您安心閱讀新聞。

Welcome to Polify (hereinafter referred to as "this App"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with applicable laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we process information so you can read news with peace of mind.

---

## 1. 我們收集與處理的資料 Types of Data We Collect

### 1.1 個人識別資料 Personal Identifiable Information (PII)
本 App **不收集**您的姓名、電話、電郵或具體的 GPS 地理位置資訊。
This App does **NOT collect** your name, phone number, email, or specific GPS location data.

### 1.2 本地儲存資料 Local Storage Data
為了提供個人化體驗與順暢的離線閱讀功能，以下資料將 **僅儲存於您的裝置本地**（透過 Hive 與 SharedPreferences），不會上傳至我們的伺服器：
* **收藏清單 (Favorites)**：您標記為喜愛的新聞。
* **訂閱設定 (Subscriptions)**：您偏好的新聞來源篩選設定。
* **搜尋紀錄 (Search History)**：您在 App 內的搜尋關鍵字。
* **應用程式設定**：包含語言偏好（中/英）與導引頁完成狀態。

For a personalized experience and offline reading, the following data is **stored locally on your device only** (via Hive & SharedPreferences) and is not uploaded to our servers:
* **Favorites**: News items you mark as liked.
* **Subscriptions**: Your preferred news source filtering settings.
* **Search History**: Keywords you searched within the App.
* **App Settings**: Including language preferences (CN/EN) and onboarding status.

### 1.3 匿名技術數據 Anonymous Technical Data
* **裝置資訊**：如設備型號、系統版本、**震動回饋 (Haptic Feedback)** 相容性。
* **效能數據**：匿名化的崩潰日誌（Crash Reports），用於修復程式錯誤。
* **Device Info**: Device model, OS version, and **Haptic Feedback** compatibility.
* **Performance Data**: Anonymized crash reports used for bug fixing.

---

## 2. 資料收集目的 Purpose of Data Use

* **內容整合**：透過官方公開 RSS 來源整合新聞資訊，僅供閱讀與導向原文。
* **操作體驗**：利用 **觸覺回饋 (Haptic Feedback)** 提升翻頁、收藏與清除數據時的互動質感。
* **意見回饋**：當您使用意見回饋功能時，會導向外部 Google 表單，您可以自願填寫。

* **Content Integration**: Aggregating news from official public RSS sources for reading and linking to original articles.
* **UX Enhancement**: Using **Haptic Feedback** to improve the tactile experience during paging, favoriting, and clearing data.
* **User Feedback**: When using the feedback feature, you will be directed to an external Google Form where you can voluntarily provide input.

---

## 3. 第三方服務與連結 Third-Party Services & Links

* **官方來源**：資料來自合法且公開的政府新聞與官方平台。
* **外部連結**：本 App 包含指向外部網站的連結。點擊「閱讀全文」後，您的隱私將受該第三方網站之政策保護。

* **Official Sources**: Data is sourced from legal and public government news platforms.
* **External Links**: This App contains links to external websites. Once you click "Read More", your privacy is governed by the policies of those third-party sites.

---

## 4. 用戶權利與資料刪除 User Rights & Data Deletion

您擁有對本地數據的絕對控制權：
* **手動清除**：您可以在 App 設定中清除所有本地快取與紀錄。
* **解除安裝**：刪除本 App 將自動移除所有儲存於本地的設定與收藏。

You have absolute control over your local data:
* **Manual Clear**: You can clear all local cache and history in the App settings.
* **Uninstall**: Deleting the App will automatically remove all local settings and favorites.
